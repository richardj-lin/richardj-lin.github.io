Competitive Analysis

(Click to open in new tab)

Why Should Google Care?

Many other messaging applications have Integrated Video Calls. Google Messages does not, which can hinder users from using the application for the lack of this feature. They may be more prone to switch to something like WhatsApp, which is slightly more inconvenient for users because it does not work with SMS/MMS/RCS messaging.
